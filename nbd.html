NBD Biblion

Goal: Possibility for high school students to find a book that fits their needs.
Tasks: UI/UX design, prototype, Visual Interface Design.

A book directory application that should accompany high school students looking for a reading book in the library that fits their needs. Task from NBD Biblion who wants to an efficient way to help high school students looking for a reading book in the library.

Problem
Lately students don't tend to visit the public library anymore and the only place where reading books are accessible for them is at the school library.


Solution
A tablet (iPad) stand that is embedded on the ground at the entrance of the school library. On this tablet runs an app where the student can find a book that fits their needs.

Concept
The student gets provided with different options to choose regarding to reading level, age and book genres to get results of a list with books. Interface that is designed with more visual elements to create a more playful ambience for the students.
