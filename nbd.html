Tesla Roadster Campaignpage

Goal: Promote the car without hardselling the product.
Tasks: UI/UX design, Webdesign, Campaign.

A campaign page of the upcoming Tesla Roadster car. Based on an intern assignment commissioned by Mediamonks with the focus on showcasing this vehicle is greater than its competition rather than trying to hardsell the product.

Assignment
Create a campaign page both desktop and mobile size. The page should not be prioritized to hard-sell the product but rather explaining why this vehicle is better than the competition.


Solution
Landingpage providing the most relevant content regarding to the vehicle. Focus on prioritizing to explain and showcase the vehicle is better than its competition and less attention to selling the product.

Concept
The student gets provided with different options to choose regarding to reading level, age and book genres to get results of a list with books. Interface that is designed with more visual elements to create a more playful ambience for the students.
