Woohah x Spotify

Goal: promoting Spotify by specific audience in an interactive way.
Methods: UI/UX design, concepting, marketeer strategies.

A hangout room managed by Spotify in Woohah festival where the user can demonstrate the Woohah x Spotify collaboration app to encourage festival visitors to take a Spotify subscription.

View demo

The goal of this concept to is encourage festival visitors to take a Spotify subscription. Spotify wants to promote their streaming service in an interactive way during a festival or event.


This concept consists of a physical (any object or area) and digital component (user interface).


For the physical component I created a hangout area, where visitors can relax from the busy crowds outside. Besides that, this hangout is built to demonstrate the application through touchscreen panels, letting the user try the application themselves encouraging them to download the app.

I merged Woohah and Spotify into one app for the digital component.


The Woohah app is a digital guide to get you through the festival during the weekend. From maps to schedules which is a must for all the visitors.


Both Woohah and Spotify manages an app and I brought out the main elements of Spotify and combined that with the Woohah app (which I created my own version of).


I saw this as a strategic opportunity to making it more attractive for users taking the subscription. By combining the two apps the user is determined to download the app in order to find its way through the festival and also has the chance to make use out of Spotify at the same time.
