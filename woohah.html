Woohah x Spotify

Goal: promoting Spotify by specific audience in an interactive way.
Methods: UI/UX design, concepting, marketeer strategies.

A hangout room managed by Spotify in Woohah festival where the user can test the Woohah x Spotify collaboration app with the purpose encouraging festival visitors to take a Spotify subscription.

View demo

The goal of this concept to is encourage festival visitors to take a Spotify subscription. Spotify has to promote their streaming service in an interactive way during a festival or event.


This concept consist of a physical (any object or area) and digital component (user interface).


For the physical component I created a hangout area, where visitors can chill or recover from the crowds outside. But the whole purpose of this hangout is to demonstrate the digital component through touchscreen panels, letting the user experience the app and hopefully urges them to download the application.

I merged Woohah and Spotify into one app for the digital component.

Both Woohah and Spotify manages an app so I decided to bring out the main elements of Spotify and merging that into the Woohah app (where I created an own version of).


The Woohah app contains all the info about the festival from maps to schedules which is a must for all the visitors.


I saw this as a strategic opportunity to make it attractive for the users taking the subscription, because by using the Woohah app you are experiencing the use of Spotify at the same time.
