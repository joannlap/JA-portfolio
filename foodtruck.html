Foodtruck Rental

Goal: possibility to rent and rent out foodtrucks through an application
Tasks: UI/UX design, prototype, information architecture.

Foodtruck Rental gives you the ability to rent and rent out foodtrucks with clients or amongst employers.

Problem
Lots of foodtruck owners rent their foodtrucks through Foodtruckbooking.com, but all the owners can agree that the website is a complete mess. The orginisation behind Rollende keukens (an annual foodtruck festival) came with the idea to develop a new appplication to compete with the website.


Solution
Create an application where you can switch between profiles as an owner of the foodtrucks or as a renter. Possibility to rent your truck through the app without the use of a website.

Concept
An application made for foodtruck owners and foodtruck renters where both parties can take advantage for their function. As an owner you have the ability to rent your truck on the app and as a renter you can easily look for foodtrucks to rent.
