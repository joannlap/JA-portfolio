2D game inspired by the manga “Promised Neverland”

In 2021 I followed the minor Applied Game Design, this was my first encounter with game design and development. The first project we were asked to build a 2D platform game in five weeks. Pressure was on, but it was hella fun. Also, this minor showed me a different approach to interaction design which is very interesting.

I was very intrigued by the storytelling of the manga “Promised Neverland” that made the starting point for my game; The Orphans. The orphans tells the story about Conny who’s one of the ten orphans that escaped from the orphanage after they found out they life in a world where kids are raised to be livestock for demons. The game starts when Conny is wandering through the creepy forest with the goal to find ingredients in order to create this recipe that needs to end the children livestock for demons. Along the way Conny has to face poisoned vegetation obstacles and demons to collect the ingredients.

This was a individual project, I partially created my own assets (character, items, UI, enemy) the rest I got through the asset store provided by Unity. Everything was build in Unity and the audio was originated from different sources.

Research

To find out what mechanics I want to use for my game, I played a few games that fits my story. The storytelling shows that the protagonist eliminates the enemy with swords and handmade weapons. Therefore I chose for sword attacks as the main mechanic. Afterwards I created moodboards regarding to the environment, level design and characters to showcase what type of ambience I wanted to implement in the game.

Design proces

Based on the moodboards and prior research, I wanted to create a dark forest setting with good amount of platforming where the character has to go through different obstacles and slay enemies. The final product

After five weeks of many iterations and testing, I built a game that at first is quite slow paced but it can get challenging later on. The goal is to survive the obstacles and enemies in order to collect all the ingredients.
