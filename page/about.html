Hope my somewhat disturbing illustration didn't scare you off, hey I'm Jo-Ann.

My interests happens to be always involved with art by absorbing, creating, and sharing it. Ever since I was a kid I enjoyed making things myself because it lives up my imagination. Studying Communication and Multimedia Design made me consciously having a better understanding for people, their needs and behaviour.

It's hard to say what I like, because of my large interests and that probably translates to my work. What I do know is that I'm passionate about experimenting with my developed skillset I gained the past years as a student, to create meaningful yet functional solutions that'll make a proper fit to the user. Fun creations can also be meaningful :).

When I'm not behind my desk, you'd most likely find me in a dance studio or wandering outside looking for foodies.
