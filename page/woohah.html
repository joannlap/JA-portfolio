Innovative experience by implementing a streaming service

For my first year project, we were asked to create an innovative experience for a music festival by implementing a streaming service. Woohah, perhaps the largest hiphop festival in Europe brings the hiphop community together in large numbers. Together with other genres, they define Spotifyâ€™s tremendous succes, however this seems not to hit the music lovers.

This application allows visitors to explore Spotify in a way of not only streaming music, but getting more engaged with their favorite artists. Leaving an immersive experience making Spotify more attractive to the Woohah visitors.

Problem statement

Where the musiclovers at?

Generally, Spotify targets to the average music streamer. Anyone who listens to music can lose themselves in the limitless catalogue of music on their device. However, there's group of musiclovers whom are almost inmersed in the artist who wants to know everything about them. They're not using the streamingservice because it lacks all kinds of information about artists, such as reviews of albums, video clips and documentaries.

Research

I conducted deskresearch and surveys regarding to the festival and the streaming service. To find out how Woohah attracts their visitors, what Spotify currently has to offer, the concurrence between different platforms and how other companies increase their brand awareness on festivals.

User goals

  • Attract new visitors to Spotify platform
  • Show that Spotify offers more than streaming music

Survey findings

  • Average visitor of Woohah festival are between 18-24 years old
  • An average day at Woohah consist of eat, sleep, drink, smoke, concerts and repeated
  • They enjoy discovering new artists during the festival besides only visiting their favorite artists
  • Lyrics, connecting more with events and information about artists are the missing features on Spotify

Ideation

Three concepts

Based on the findings through research, I created three concepts that possibly can catch the visitors attention during the festival. By implementing a physical object in the festival combined with a digital application, leaving an immersive experience. At the end it turned out to be a combination of the first two concepts.

Look & Feel

Combine both brand identities

The idea of creating a combination of both brand identities is to engage a famiiar sense of style to the user. By implementing Woohah's brand identity into's Spotify's platform. The user should be able to recognize Spotify's design pattern in Woohah's brand identity.

Solution

During my research I discovered that companies are quite creative when it comes to raising brand awareness during events. This encouraged me to be more creative and to look beyond your typical marketing strategies. Something Woohah visitors would appreciate. Survey findings stated that people miss engagements from festivals in the streaming platform. Which is why I created a collaboration app of Spotify x Woohah by merging the two platforms. Everything about the festival at one glance and the ability to search in the limitless library of songs in Spotify. To me this is the most subtle way to promote a brand without explicitly trying to engage people use your service or product.

Everything about the festival at one glance

Stay connected to the festival, anything you need to know regarding to Woohah is showcased on the app. Timetables, stages, about artists.

Personalized playlist

Based on which artist you visited during the festival, Spotify creates personalized playlists from each day during the festival.
