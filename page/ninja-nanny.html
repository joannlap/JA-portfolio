2D platformer game for the kids tv show Ninja Nanny

The second season of the kids tv show Ninja Nanny aires on the dutch channel NPO Zapp after summer of 2021. Students of the minor Applied Game Design were asked by the writers from this tv show to build a game to hype the kids for the new season during the summer.

This was a team project, were I and another were responsible for creating the assets in the game (the visuals). All the assets were build in Unity and drawn on Procreate.

Research

Target group

Kids between 8 and 12 years old is our target audience for this project. We did prior research to find what kids are up to these days regarding to games.

  • Kids are getting more independent and like solving problems
  • They are looking for more challenge
  • They feel outgrowned to "kids" products
