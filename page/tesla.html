Digital campaign for Tesla Roadster

Assignment

When I applied for an interaction design intern at MediaMonks, I received a short assignment to see how my approach is to digital projects. Create a campaign page both desktop and mobile size. The page should not be prioritized to hard-sell the product but rather explaining why this vehicle is better than the competition.

Research

Getting an idea what type of setting and design language I wanted to deliver, I made a collection of different car web designs and created a moodboard.

Ideation

I wanted to catch the users attention by adding an animation on the landingpage. This could be an interesting introduction to the new vehicle. Based on this idea I started to sketch wireframes getting a grasp impression of the concept.

Look & feel

There wasn't much to be added to the styleguide since Tesla has a clear color palette and brand identity.

Iterations

At first I wanted to keep the style of the current Tesla site. I only separated the car out of the background to make it move in the animation. Therefore I kept the same elements out of the current website. But

End Result

I tried to present the car not focussing hard selling product, instead of a call to action for ‘order now’ I replaced it with "Discover" button. Catch the users attention yet not forcing buying the car. Kept it simple, not bombarding the user with specs and info that aren't relevant. Enhance the beauty of the car through experimental visuals.
