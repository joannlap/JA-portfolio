Loadmap

Goal: Improve the usability of booking a charging station in Amsterdam through an application.
Tasks: UI/UX design, prototype.

Loadmap is an helpful map to your next electric vehicle charging station.

Problem
How can we design a solution for 2030 that also takes technology into consideration to allow Amsterdam citizens to easily book a charging station and to allow them to charge their EV? - 10Clouds


Solution
An application where Amsterdam citizens can book a charging station through an application. Showcasing the available spots in the area, the amount of spots available at that area and the route directions to the station.

In-App charging pass
This intregated charging pass displays the duration of charging the verhicle, history of charges, duration and cost. This is the same method as how Apple Pay works through face-ID when you hold the phone against the charging station.
